More About Me

I graduated from Texas A & M University with a BA in International Studies and English. I currently work full-time in the insurance world.

One of my favorite things about any kind of work opportunity is getting to work on a team that is always striving for improvement through innovations and ideas. I love the creativity that comes from working with peers and mentors, which always has me thinking outside the box.

I have been interested in learning about computers and coding for a long time. Thinkful has offered me the opportunity to dive into web development. My interest and passion grows with each lesson successfully completed. I love knowing that I will never be bored and never be done learning.

I love a good puzzle and a few tough problems or mysteries to solve! I always love learning something new, whether it's a new recipe or ingredient or trying out a new crafting skill. And I would never turn down the opportunity to relax with good friends and family over a cup of coffee or a margarita!

I am excited to put my growing skills to use in web development.
